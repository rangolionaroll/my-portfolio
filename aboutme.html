About Me

Hi, I'm Rangoli! I'm a Game Designer and Developer with a background in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Throughout my entire life I have been fascinated with both science and art. In highschool, I found the intersection of both of them in STEAM, and shaped my education around it. Along with the technical courses for my engineering degree, I enjoyed taking electives like psychology and animation to further my understanding of the human experience.

What I aspire to do now is to create immersive and meaningful experiences using both art and technology. I love sharing my knowledge and learning from others and am constantly looking to try new things!
