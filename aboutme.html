About Me

Hi, I'm Rangoli! I'm a Game Designer and Developer with a background in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Throughout my entire life I have been fascinated with both science and art. In highschool, I found the intersection of both of them in STEAM, and shaped my education around it. Along with the technical courses for my engineering degree, I enjoyed taking electives like psychology and animation to further my understanding of the human experience. Sticking to my affinity for both art and logic I discovered my passion for creating games and immersive experiences, particularly ones that are meant for learning and thinking.

I got my start in game development while in college and have been fortunate to be able to experience many roles from art and design to programming and development, and even some event planning and production. While I have been able to try many things, I found my niche as a Technical Game Designer, being able to bridge the gap between design and development. While being able to understand why a design is created with a user in mind, and understanding how a game might be programmed, I enjoy finding solutions for both design and development.

What I aspire to do now is to create immersive and meaningful experiences using both art and technology. I love sharing my knowledge and learning from others and am constantly looking to try new things!
