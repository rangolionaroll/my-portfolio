Arbor Trail Adventure

An augmented reality experience designed to showcase the Arbor Trail at the Rutgers University Inn and Conference Center.

Roles: Lead Developer, Designer

Introduction


This project was designed as an interactive way to learn about the plants and animals on the Arbor Trail. This, being my first project as a student programmer at Rutgers GRID, was a huge part of my game development and design education. I worked very closely with the lead designer in the making of this project and have gone on to give presentations about the iterative design process used in this game. Below are some screenshots from a workshop presentation I did for an introductory game design course.


Design & Development



    Working with the landscaping group that was restoring this trail, we came up with showcase goals and designed based on those. Some of those goals included:

  • Showcase the wildlife on trail, including plants and animals.
  • Use augmented reality technology to enhance the experience.
  • Create signage for the trail so it is informative even without the app.

    • With these goals in mind we came up with a lot of really cool ideas, such as showing 3D versions of the wildlife and wildlife through the seasons, which we narrowed down for our first playable. We decided to focus on a trivia mechanic to give facts about each of the plants once the player scans the sign for the plant. Our current version of the game focuses on the trivia mechanic but has an added story to immerse the players in a role. They are adventurers walking the trail when they come upon a book with missing pages as they walk through the trail and answer the trivia, they restore pages to the book. Screenshots of the current game are shown to the left, including two videos of the scanning and trivia mechanisms.

    As the developer of this project, I built out and programmed the game in Unity, including but not limited to the features listed below. For the AR portion of the game I used Vuforia.

  • UI Menu and Buttons
  • Responsive UI
  • UI Transitions and Animations
  • Collection Event System
  • Locked and Unlocked State Tracking
  • Save System
  • Templatized trivia and information pages - for future updates with more items to collect
