Rutgers Day 2021

This project was designed for Virtual Rutgers Day 2021.

Roles: Designer, Social Media Coordinator

Introduction

This project was designed as an online activity for Rutgers Day 2021. With Rutgers Day being held online, we wanted to host a family friendly activity. In the past, Rutgers GRID had used Minecraft for a Rutgers 250 event, so we had a lot of assets that were already created. Due to a short timeline we decided to set up a Minecraft server to showcase the campus. With our target audience being young children who may already use Minecraft, I worked with the team to come up with activities and also organized the social media marketing.

Activities

    While many of the activities were being reused from the original Minecraft project, one of the ones I changed was the scavenger hunt. For anyone who had not been to the campus before, or would not be able to recognize the buildings by name, a list of things to find may not be as fun.

    To show off how amazing the campus was modeled, I decided to create a scavenger hunt by finding images of the campus online and showing that exact view in Minecraft. I created a document showing all of the original images as the challenge, and then showed their Minecraft counterparts in a gallery. This gallery also had information on where it could be found in the Minecraft server just incase someone had trouble finding it.

    This created a super simple scavenger hunt which allowed us to fully show off all the campus features we captured in Minecraft. And it would also be fun to try to find the spot the picture was taken from.

Social Media Content

Along with helping design the experience, I coordinated the social media marketing content. This included outlining the posts, the timeline for posting, the screenshots and eye catching images, accompanying captions, and alternative text to make the images more accessible.

I kept one image the same to provide information on how to access the event and then created individual posts to showcase features and activities. Some of these individual graphics are shown below.

    Get Ready, Minecraft Rutgers is back and better than ever! Originally made for Rutgers 250th, now with brand new features. Be there and be square!

    Can you find all the locations in Minecraft? Check out our Minecraft Scavenger Hunt on Rutgers Day!

    Rutgers Day 2021 is today! Don't forget to visit us in Minecraft. Can't wait to see you there!

    Thank you for stopping by last weekend for Rutgers Day! If you didn't get a chance to jump in the world, don't worry! Our website and Minecraft server are still up for you to explore.
