As a member of the Rutgers University Seeing Eye Puppy Raising Club for 3 years, I was able to help raise many future guide dogs. My involvement in the program included doing basic obedience training, taking the puppies to classes and events, and exposing them to a variety of new environments. I hope to be able to raise a Seeing Eye puppy of my own in the near future.

Read about some of my experiences below!

Andy

Andy was a German shepherd that I started sitting when he was 3 months old. I was able to take him to my Linear Signals and Systems, and 2D Animation classes on a weekly basis. Being able to sit him regularly, I was able to take notice of things that made him anxious, and slowly expose him to more stimulating environments. We went to events like corn mazes, basketball games, movies, marathons and many more. I was even able to take him to NYC for a weekend and get him used to an environment with constant noise.
