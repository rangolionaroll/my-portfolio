2019

In 2019, I was able to spend spring break helping my mom, Tejaswini, a piano artist for Sing For Hope. I spent hours everyday in a studio in NYC, painting a piano bench to accompany her piano. This involved mimicking her style of painting and doing tiny, detailed work.
