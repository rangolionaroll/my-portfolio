I first learned how to crochet when I was in highschool. I have picked it up again in the past year to make presents for my friends.

Check out my recent crochet projects!

Kirby Blanket

This is a crochet blanket I made as a baby present. I remade a pixel art pattern of Kirby to be suitable for corner to corner crocheting. After completing the pixel art portion of the blanket, I wanted to add a border and chose stars to fit the theme. I first did a round of single crochet stitches around the edge. Then, to make sure the stars were well defined, I made 40 stars and stitched them into the blanket. Afterwards, I did a round of slip stitches around the edge to connect the arms and define the shape of the stars.
