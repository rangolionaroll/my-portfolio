Rutgers Game Research and Immersive Design (GRID) is a department in Rutgers Division of Continuing Studies, that works with faculty, staff and the Rutgers community. GRID explores new technologies and game design to build innovative games and immersive experiences to improve education.

I currently work at GRID as a Game Designer and Developer with a focus in XR technologies.
